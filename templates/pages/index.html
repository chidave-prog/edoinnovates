WHO WE ARE
We are an innovation centre creating the supply of highly skilled ICT talent in Edo State and improving greater access to jobs in the ICT sector. We are located in the heart of Edo state, Benin City.
Edoinnovates was launched on 14th June, 2020 by his Excellency, Vice President Yemi Osinbajo and has since inception, seen over 5000 beneficiaries.
    WHAT WE DO
  • Equip Youth with high demand ICT skill trainings and connect them to jobs in the ICT sector
  • Provide trainings and business expansion opportunities for startups through incubation and acceleration programs
  • Provide Co-working spaces, training halls, fully equipped ICT labs with fast internet access for trainings, seminars and boot camps
  • Provide new and existing businesses with Admin support, Accommodation, mentoring and Tech support services
  • Growing the Tech community in Edo State
Jobs

‘Edo Innovates’ is a sublet of the Edo State Skills Development Agency (ESSDA) set up by his Excellency Governor Godwin Obaseki with the aim to connect Youths to Jobs in the 4th industrial revolution; leveraging Technology for Job Creation and Enterprise Development. more

The Edo Innovation Hub serves as the home for technology in the south-south, equipping youths in Edo with high demand ICT and Entrepreneurship skill trainings with state of the art facilities, in a serene and conducive environment that sparks innovative ideas. less

Start-Ups

The Edo Innovation Hub was designed to help the very best founders and start-ups by facilitating access to high quality work spaces and infrastructure, Teaching and Learning, Early stage investment capital, Investor and Peer networks. more

Edo Innovation hub is positioned at improving the livelihood of Edo residents through different innovation programs and trainings that leads to employment and entrepreneurship opportunities in the State. less

Partnerships

The hub has collaborated with National and International partners like Amazon, Google Africa, Curators University, LinkedIn, CADD center, GIZ, Oxfam International, HPLife, Center for women development, Facebook, Gidimobile and many more

more to deliver cutting edge ICT skill trainings, solutions and connecting beneficiaries to opportunities and jobs in the IT sector. less
