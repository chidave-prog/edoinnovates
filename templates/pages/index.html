WHO WE ARE?

Edo Innovates, is a cluster for technology, inventors, entrepreneurs situated in the hearts of Benin-City, Edo State. Launched on the 14th of June 2018 by his Excellency, Vice President Yemi Osinbanjo, the hub has seen over 5000 beneficiaries since its inception.

WHAT WE DO
  • Up skill youths with ICT Skills with connect them to jobs in the tech sector
  • Provide training and business expansion opportunities for startups and small businesses
  • Provide co-working spaces, training halls, internet and other services to businesses within Edo
  • Provide new and existing businesses with Admin Support, Accommodation, Mentoring and Tech Support services.
  • Fully equipped ICT Labs with fast internet access for ICT trainings, seminars and boot camps.
Jobs

The Edo Innovates is a sublet of the Skill Development Center (Edojobs) set up by his Excellency Governor Godwin Obaseki, the hub sits at the heart of Benin City with an aim to seeks/address the problem of providing jobs for the teeming residents of Edo by seeking innovation in ICT and Technology.

Start-Ups
